Our Story ❤️

MOMENT I

I still remember that day as if it were a scene frozen in time — 19th September, 2023, under the soft chaos of Ganesh Chaturthi, when we were all practicing for our dance performance.

MOMENT II

The evening had that strange magic in it... During a break, we joked that somewhere inside all of us, there was someone we secretly carried in our hearts. And in that exact moment, we both looked at each other with that awkward, suspicious little connection that only later makes sense.

MOMENT III

Then our friends decided we should dance in that romantic way, the kind where you’re close enough to feel the silence between two heartbeats. You were wearing that pink dress, and though I was paired with someone else first, something felt distant, unreal. Then the pair changed, and suddenly it was you.

MOMENT IV

The moment I held your hips and we began to sway, the world seemed to slow down. I looked into your eyes and felt a sudden warmth, a trembling in my chest, butterflies rushing through me like they had been waiting all along for this one moment. It felt like love, simple and beautiful.

MOMENT V

I remember standing too close for that photo, as if the air already knew our story was beginning. After all the laughter and the heartbeat-hidden glances, we sat on the sofa trying to act normal when nothing about that evening felt normal anymore.

MOMENT VI

The next day, by the lift, the truth finally found its voice. We confessed to each other, both scared, both hoping. I was afraid you wouldn’t say my name. But you did.

TODAY

From that moment on, our story began — and everything since then has felt like a film I never want to end.
